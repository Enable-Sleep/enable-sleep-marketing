## EXAMPLE BRIEF #1: Blog Post on Standard of Care

### Primary Marketing Theme
1. ✅ The "Standard of Care" Opportunity (ADA 2017 recommendation)

### Target Audience Stage
✅ Awareness (just learning about DSM opportunity)

### Content Type
✅ Blog Post (1,200-2,000 words)

### Key Message
"The ADA's 2017 recommendation didn't just suggest screening—it established a new standard of care that creates both a professional obligation and a competitive advantage for dentists."

### Desired Action (CTA)
✅ Download resource ("Complete Guide to Meeting the ADA 2017 Standard")

### Required Elements
Must include these specific items:
- [x] Specific statistic: 80% undiagnosed rate, meaning most dentists aren't meeting standard
- [x] Dr. Zelk signature concept: "Sleep suffocation" and signs dentists already see
- [x] Case study/example: Brief example of dentist who implemented screening
- [x] Other: Reference to liability/compliance angle

### Avoid/Don't Include
- Don't fear-monger about liability
- Don't make it sound difficult to implement
- Don't position as "another thing to do"—frame as opportunity

### SEO Keywords
Primary: ADA dental sleep medicine screening standard of care
Secondary: dentist sleep apnea screening guidelines 2017

### Additional Context
This will be pillar content for the blog. Should establish authority on the topic and be comprehensive enough to reference in other content pieces. Tone should be educator helping colleague understand important professional development, not lawyer warning about compliance.

## EXAMPLE BRIEF #2: Email for Sequence

### Primary Marketing Theme
5. ✅ Remove the Barriers That Keep 99% of Dentists Out

### Target Audience Stage
✅ Consideration (interested but unsure how to start)

### Content Type
✅ Email (300-600 words)

### Key Message
"100,000 dentists have been trained in dental sleep medicine, but fewer than 250 successfully get paid. The problem isn't the training—it's the implementation barriers no one removed until now."

### Desired Action (CTA)
✅ Schedule practice assessment (15-minute call)

### Required Elements
Must include these specific items:
- [x] Specific statistic: 100,000 trained, 250 succeed stat
- [x] Dr. Zelk signature concept: "Removing barriers to care"
- [x] Case study/example: Quick mention of common barrier (billing complexity)
- [x] Other: What Enable Sleep specifically provides

### Avoid/Don't Include
- Don't overwhelm with all the barriers at once
- Don't make existing training programs sound bad
- Don't promise it's "easy"—promise it's "supported"

### SEO Keywords
Not applicable for email

### Additional Context
This is Email #3 in a 7-email nurture sequence. Previous emails established the epidemic (Email 1) and dentist's unique position (Email 2). This email pivots to acknowledging why they haven't started yet, validating those concerns, then showing Enable Sleep is the solution. Next email will focus on Dr. Zelk's credibility.
