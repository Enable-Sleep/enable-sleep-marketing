## Content Structure Formula

### Opening (Hook)
- Compelling question
- Shocking statistic  
- Personal story
- Dentist's reality

### Body (Problem → Solution)
1. Problem statement (make it visceral)
2. Evidence/Impact (use statistics)
3. Why it's been hard (acknowledge barriers)
4. How Enable Sleep fixes it
5. What success looks like

### Closing (CTA)
- **Soft:** Continue learning
- **Medium:** Explore solutions
- **Strong:** Schedule assessment

## Voice Checklist (✅ = Good / ❌ = Bad)

✅ Educator sharing insights  
✅ "We" language (inclusive)  
✅ Celebrates dentistry's potential  
✅ Acknowledges legitimate barriers  
✅ Specific clinical examples  
✅ Urgency through facts  

❌ Marketing jargon/hype  
❌ Blaming dentists  
❌ Fear-mongering  
❌ Unrealistic promises  
❌ Dentists vs. physicians positioning  
❌ Corporate/sales speak  

## The Ultimate Test

**Before publishing, ask:**
> "Does this sound like Dr. Zelk teaching a colleague over coffee, 
> or does it sound like marketing copy?"

If it's the latter, rewrite.
