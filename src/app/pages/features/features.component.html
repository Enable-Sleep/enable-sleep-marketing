How the Enable Sleep Workflow Connects Everything

Dental sleep medicine involves coordination across screening, diagnosis, treatment, and billing—and most practices struggle because these steps live in separate systems. Enable Sleep connects them into a single, guided workflow.

When a patient screens positive for sleep-related breathing disorders, the platform creates a case that follows them through every step. Referrals to sleep physicians are tracked automatically. Home sleep test results flow back into the patient record. Impressions, device orders, and delivery visits are documented with our AI Medical Scribe, which generates compliant SOAP notes and billing codes for each visit type.

Your patients interact through a dedicated patient portal where they complete intake forms, track their treatment progress, and communicate with your care team. Behind the scenes, HIPAA-compliant security and encrypted data storage protect every record.

The result: your team spends less time on paperwork, your compliance risk drops, and you can focus on what matters—helping patients breathe better at night.
