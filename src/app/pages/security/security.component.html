Why Security Matters for Dental Sleep Medicine Software

Dental sleep medicine involves sensitive health data that falls under HIPAA regulations: sleep study results, medical diagnoses, treatment records, and billing information. Any platform handling this data must meet strict security and compliance standards—not as a nice-to-have, but as a legal requirement.

Enable Sleep is built from the ground up for healthcare. Every component of the platform—from AI-generated clinical documentation to the patient portal—operates within a HIPAA-compliant infrastructure with end-to-end encryption, role-based access controls, and comprehensive audit logging.
