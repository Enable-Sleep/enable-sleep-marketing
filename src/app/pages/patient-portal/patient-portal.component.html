Why Patient Engagement Matters in Dental Sleep Medicine

Oral appliance therapy requires more patient touchpoints than most dental procedures. Between screening questionnaires, sleep test coordination, device fittings, and compliance follow-ups, patients who disengage midway represent lost clinical outcomes and lost revenue. The Enable Sleep patient portal keeps patients connected and informed at every stage.

Digital intake forms capture sleep history, medical questionnaires, and consent documents before the patient arrives, saving your front desk 15–20 minutes per visit. Automated appointment reminders and compliance check-ins reduce no-shows and keep treatment on track. Patients can message your care team with questions about their appliance, review their sleep test results, and track their treatment milestones—all from their phone.

The portal works hand-in-hand with the Enable Sleep platform features and the AI Medical Scribe, creating a seamless experience from screening through long-term compliance monitoring. And because it's a Progressive Web App, your patients don't need to download anything from an app store—they just open a link and add it to their home screen.
