Built for Dental Sleep Medicine, Not Generic Notes

Unlike general-purpose medical scribes, Enable Sleep's AI is trained specifically for dental sleep medicine workflows. It understands the clinical terminology, screening criteria, and billing codes that matter for oral appliance therapy—from Mallampati scores and Epworth Sleepiness Scale results to ICD-10 codes like G47.33 and CPT codes like E0486.

The AI Scribe supports over seven distinct visit types used in dental sleep medicine: new patient consultations, sleep test reviews, impression appointments, device deliveries, adjustment visits, follow-up evaluations, and compliance checks. Each visit type has its own documentation template, ensuring that your notes meet payer requirements and medical billing standards every time.

Documentation integrates directly with the Enable Sleep platform, feeding SOAP notes into the patient record and triggering the next step in the workflow. Patients can review their treatment plans through the patient portal, and your billing team gets clean, coded notes ready for medical claims submission.
